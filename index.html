Enjoy An Exceptional Journey of Taste

At Icon Restaurant, we’re more than just a meal—we’re a celebration of Ugandan heritage served in a space that feels like home. Tucked away in the heart of Jinja, our inviting indoor and lush outdoor dining areas offer the perfect atmosphere for every occasion.

Indulge in the bold flavors of traditional Ugandan Snacks, from Pizzas and buggers to perfectly grilled Chicken. Each bite tells the story of our passion for authentic cuisine, hospitality, and the soulful tastes that make Uganda truly unique.
